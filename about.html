Intro

Hi there! My name is Terrance and I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer based in southern California. I've always had a deep curiosity for the way things work and I'm also a creative. Programming has allowed me a perfect medium to express both outlets and to date I've worked on over 15 projects with over 20 engineers total.

When I'm not hacking away at the computer I'm doing my second favorite thing, helping others! In my past career field part of my job entailed me instructing medical field students and it is here I discovered I have a knack for teaching and communication.

Other interests of mine include researching, exercising, reading, video games, drinking a hot cup of macha, and fiddling around on the keyboard (the music kind).
